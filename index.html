Rules:

  • Time Limit: You have 60 seconds to complete the challenge.
  • Lives: You have 4 chances to make a mistake.
  • Controls: Use the left, right, or up arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate.
  • Objective: Reach the end of the challenge before time runs out. Avoid obstacles and make strategic moves.
  • Failures: If you make a wrong move or hit an obstacle, you lose one life. Lose all 4 lives, and the game ends.

Have Fun: The primary goal is to enjoy the game while facing the time pressure. Keep it simple, enjoy the challenge, and have fun!
